Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Medicuity

How long does implementation take?

Most healthcare organizations are up and running within 48 hours. Our team handles the technical setup while you focus on your patients.

Is Medicuity HIPAA compliant?

Yes, we are fully HIPAA compliant with SOC 2 Type II certification. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit, and we maintain strict access controls.

What EHR systems do you integrate with?

We integrate with 50+ major EHR systems including Epic, Cerner, Allscripts, athenahealth, and NextGen. Custom integrations are also available.

Can I try Medicuity before committing?

Absolutely! We offer a 30-day free trial with full access to our platform. No credit card required to get started.
